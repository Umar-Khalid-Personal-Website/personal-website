Personal Dessert Book

These are three of my favorite deserts.

Tiramisu

Tiramisu is a coffee-flavoured Italian dessert. raditional tiramisu uses raw egg yolks and sugar beaten and then combined with raw whipped egg whites and mascarpone.

Tres Leches Cake

A tres leches cake is a sponge cake that is soaked in three kinds of milk. It is soft, perfectly moist, and not soggy. While Tres Leches cake was first made and served in Latin America, it still has its ties all the way to Europe.

Mahalabia

It is is a creamy Middle Eastern milk pudding garnished with nuts, rose petals and/or a sugar syrup. It is also known as "Muhallebi" in Turkey.
